Richard Hendricks

Richard Hendricks (played by Thomas Middleditch) from the TV series Silicon Valley was the CEO and founder of Pied Piper as well as PiperNet, and is currently a Galvin Belson Professor of Ethics of Technology at Stanford. Before founding Pied Piper, Richard was an employee of Hooli.

  • Richard is an intelligent coder, great at what he does.
  • Shy genius vibe: He's brilliant and it contrasts to his inability to function in normal human situations.
  • He is fiercely dedicated to his company and vision.
